## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an **end-to-end interactive sales analytics dashboard** built in Power BI for **Blinkit** — India's last-minute grocery delivery app. The dashboard analyzes **9,000+ products** across multiple outlet types, city tiers, and product categories to uncover actionable business insights.

> **Goal:** Help category managers and operations teams identify which outlet types, locations, and product categories to prioritize for growth and inventory planning.

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

| Insight | Finding |
|---|---|
| 📍 City Tier Performance | Tier 3 cities lead with **$472K** in sales, outperforming Tier 1 ($336K) |
| 🏪 Best Outlet Type | Supermarket Type 1 drives **65% of total revenue** ($787K across 5,577 items) |
| 🥗 Consumer Preference | **64.6% of sales** are Low Fat products — health-conscious buying is mainstream |
| 📈 Sales Peak | Outlet sales peaked at **$205K in 2018**, followed by a decline — signaling market maturity |
| ⭐ Customer Satisfaction | Average rating is consistently **3.92** across all outlet types |
| 💰 Total Revenue | **$1.2M** total sales across all outlets with an average sale of **$140.99** |

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- **KPI Cards** — Total Sales, Average Sales, Total Items, Average Rating at a glance
- **Dynamic Filter Panel** — Filter by Outlet Location Type, Outlet Size, and Item Type
- **Fat Content Analysis** — Donut chart + breakdown by outlet tier
- **Item Type Performance** — Horizontal bar chart across 16 product categories
- **Outlet Size Breakdown** — Medium (37%), High (42%), Small (20%)
- **Outlet Location Comparison** — Tier 1 vs Tier 2 vs Tier 3 performance
- **Time Series Trend** — Outlet establishment sales from 2010–2022
- **Outlet Type Summary Table** — Sales, items, avg sales, avg rating by outlet type
- **Cross-filtering** — All visuals interact with each other dynamically

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

1. **Expand Tier 3 presence** — these cities show higher sales volume and likely lower competition
2. **Stock more Low Fat variants** — 64.6% sales share indicates strong and growing demand
3. **Invest in Supermarket Type 1 format** — highest revenue and item count by far
4. **Investigate the post-2018 sales dip** — could indicate pricing, competition, or supply chain issues worth addressing
